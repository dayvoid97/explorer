# A Primer on Monad

## Introduction

Monad’s Mission states, “is to power a new world economy, suitable for a world that is increasingly online and onchain, through state-of-the-art financial infrastructure via the pillars of"

1. The Best Developer Experience : Seamless EVM compatibility and awesome initiates like Monad Madness, [Monad Founder Residency](https://residency.monad.xyz/) connect developers and founders in the Monad ecosystem to investors and businesses to build new blockchain-based services. Monad also has impressive physical locations where builders can meetup and discuss the next big ideas. Visit [The Studio](https://x.com/thestudio_xyz).
2. Global Reach & Impact: Monad aims to bring its technology and tools across the world and highlights the growth of blockchain-based payments system in Latin America and enterprise-level products in SouthEast Asia.
3. Best in-class Technology: "Technical innovations like a custom database (MonadDB) and execution optimizations (Optimistic Parallel Execution) have allowed Monad to achieve the speed necessary for mainstream applications," states Monad's mission statement. Monad's tech was built to enable for High frequency trading and other consumer apps that require maximum transaction throughput. **Monad's 10,000 TPS is a rock-base for EVM compatibility as Ethereum has the biggest developer ecosystem and they can tap into the Monad ecosystem seamlessly.**

## Monad Public Sale & Airdrop Distribution

**MON Circulating Supply**: MON is Monad's native tokens for conducting transactions and staking for generating yields. The total MON supply is 100 Billion.

**MON Token Public Sale**: 7.5 percent of the 100 billion token supply was allocated for public sale at the price of $0.025 per MON via the Coinbase token sale platform. [Learn More](https://www.monad.xyz/announcements/monads-mission-why-monad)

**MON Token Airdrop**: The MON Airdrop made available 4.73B MON tokens to be claimed across a total of 289K eligible accounts. In total, 3.33B MON tokens (~70.4%) were claimed. [Learn About MON Airdrop](https://www.monad.xyz/announcements/the-mon-airdrop-results)
