## Monad Public Sale & Airdrop Distribution

**MON Circulating Supply**: MON is Monad's native tokens for conducting transactions and staking for generating yields. The total MON supply is 100 Billion.

**MON Token Public Sale**: 7.5 percent of the 100 billion token supply was allocated for public sale at the price of $0.025 per MON via the Coinbase token sale platform. [Learn More](https://www.monad.xyz/announcements/monads-mission-why-monad)

**MON Token Airdrop**: The MON Airdrop made available 4.73B MON tokens to be claimed across a total of 289K eligible accounts. In total, 3.33B MON tokens (~70.4%) were claimed. [Learn About MON Airdrop](https://www.monad.xyz/announcements/the-mon-airdrop-results)

## Check Monad Blockchain and MON Token Data

Check MON Token Charts and Monad Blockchain Stats via [GMonads] (https://www.gmonads.com/charts). GMonads website is navigable on 4 sections: Staking, VValidators, and Charts.

- Staking : Stake your MON tokens to earn a 15.41% APY. This could change anytime. As Monad is a Proof of Stake (POS) mechanism, staking secures the network and insures the validators have a financial incentive to act honestly. Currently 12.8 billion MON tokens are staked, represeing a 12.8% of total MON supply of 100 billion.
- Validators: The Validators tab gives a list of all the public validators of the Monad blockchain ecosystem. Currently there are 164 validators. While staking MON tokens, you need to choose a validator and stake with them. Monad blockchain has a 25 MON reward per block generation and validators take a 10% cut of each block reward, meaning 22.5 MON is distributed among the stakes who choose a validator to stake.
